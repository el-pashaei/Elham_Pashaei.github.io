Brief Biography

I received my PhD degree in Computer Engineering from Yildiz Technical University in 2017. I have pursued my activity as an independent researcher after graduating. My research interests include bioinformatics, machine learning, splice site detection, RNA-seq analysis and Evolutionary Algorithms.
